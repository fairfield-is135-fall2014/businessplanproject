    This section of the Competitors Guide is intended to help you plan out your work for the competition. However, the information given here is no replacement for your mentors and other experienced advisors.


    Roadmap

    February 1st (Saturday): Team Kickstart Workshop
    February 7th Team Formation Deadline February 11th, 20th, and 25th (Tuesday/Thursday): Informational events with subject matter experts March 6th Mentor Review Sessions March 18th (Tuesday): Practice Pitch Sessions with Mentors (must have preliminary draft plan) March 30th (Sunday): Semifinal Round (must have complete plan, though revisions can be made before final round) Early April Deadline for final plan revisions April 15th (Tuesday): Finale - Cash prizes to be announced at the Gala Event


    The best teams have a plan for each event, working to get the maximum benefit from the business connections, mentoring, funding, and other resources available at the time.
